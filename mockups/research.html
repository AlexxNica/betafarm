    Mozilla is a major force in the development of the Web's fundamental technologies.

    But major additions or changes require significant research and experimentation before they can be brought to fruition. We're deeply invested in and commited to the Web and therefore willing to take on long-term research projects, even on a speculative basis.

    As programming languages and protocols are some of the universal building blocks of the web, we have projects focused on improving existing languages like Javascript, as well as design work focused on brand new languages.
